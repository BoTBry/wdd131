About Onitsha

Onitsha is a bustling commercial city located on the eastern bank of the Niger River in Anambra State, Nigeria. Known for its rich culture and economic vitality, the city is one of the most densely populated areas in southeastern Nigeria.

Demographics & Statistics

  • Population: Over 1.4 million (2023 estimate)
  • Languages Spoken: Igbo (primary), English (official)
  • Major Industries: Trade, Transportation, Manufacturing, Retail
  • Literacy Rate: Approx. 88%

Local Attractions

  • Ochanja Market – One of the largest markets in West Africa
  • River Niger Bridge – Iconic symbol and major transportation link
  • Onitsha Mall – A hub for modern shopping and entertainment
  • Obi of Onitsha’s Palace – Cultural heritage and royal seat
